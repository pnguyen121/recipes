Chicken Alfredo

Description

This recipe is for the best chicken alfredo you have ever had, rated by 10 michelin chefs. We will break down this top secret recipe step by step so you can create a beautiful dish for yourself and your friends. This recipe will require specific ingredients that will be listed below. Get these before starting your cooking.!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Penne Pasta
  • Alfredo Sauce

Directions

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add pasta to boiling water
  3. Start baking the chicken at 400 degrees in the oven
  4. After pasta is cooked strain out the water
  5. Add the alfredo sauce back into the pot with the noodles
  6. Take out the chicken and cut it up into slices
  7. Add the chicken in the pot
  8. Stir it all up
  9. Add seasonings like salt and pepper as needed
